About Me

Bosire Nyamori is an Advocate of the High Court of Kenya and a Certified Public Accountant, with over two decades of experience spanning academia, tax consulting, and legal practice.

His professional journey has allowed him to work at the intersection of law, taxation, accounting, and public policy. His core competencies include research, writing and analysis, analytical thinking, problem-solving and communication of complex policy, legal and tax issues.

His forte is tax policy and law, although he has a continuing interest in budget policy and law, the interaction between law and accounting, and the intersection between taxation, trade and investments.

He is an active writer and public commentator. He regularly publishes journal articles, op-eds, and case commentaries, and is often invited to speak at professional conferences, judicial seminars, and academic forums in Kenya and abroad.
